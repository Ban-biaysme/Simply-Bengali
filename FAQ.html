Frequently Asked Questions



#1 How can I make a Booking for a event?
We accept booking for parties of four or larger. Please use our Booking Form or email us at mailto:name@example.com" or call us at 04-477 2226.
#2 Do you offer non-spicy dishes as well?
Many Bengali dishes call for very hot spices.Please let us know if you do not like spicy food when ordering your meal. We are able to de-spice the dishes according to your preferences.
#3 What is DEKCHI SUPPLY food delivery?
In DEKCHI SUPPLY, You can order in advance, for us to deliver and serve out to your guests. This service is designed for a minimum of 30 to 75 heads approximately.
#4 What happens if I'm running late?
Call the restaurant. We will hold your booking for at least twenty minutes before reassigning your table.
#5 How much do I have to pay for my booking?
Absolutely nothing! All bookings made through Restaurant Hub are free to the consumer.
#6 Do you have parking?
Yes! Simply Bengali have their own parking and as well as parking just across the street.
#7 How many people attend an Event or a Parties?
Our average public Event or Party accommodates 30-40 guests.
