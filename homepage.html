SERVICES

From our kitchen to your table.

Food Delivery

Simply Bengali offers an extensive FREE food delivery service spread right across the cities. A minimum order value has to be ordered with our restaurant. A unique add-on feature of our food delivery is DEKCHI SUPPLY. You can order in advance, for us to deliver and serve out to your guests. This service is designed for a minimum of 30 to 75 heads approximately.

Catering

Simply Bengali has built up an enviable list of individual, and social clientele over a period of time. Each assignment is customized to suit the requirements of the host and the invitees. We do our best to create the right ambience, keeping in mind the needs of the host and his invitee list to present a happy culinary experience, rather than just serving out food.

Dine-In

Whether be it formal or casual, family time or private time, Our Simply Bengali restaurant gives you the perfect ambience to spend few hours of peace, fun and exotic dining within the homely set up.

About Us

Who we are...

  • Our Story

    The journey started in 2010 as a tiny restaurant created by a bunch of foodies for some more foodie friends. We are proud to say it still remains a Restaurant by the foodies, to the foodies and for the foodies.


    Simply Bengali is Authentic Bengali Cuisine having variety of fish, chicken & mutton dishes in service.Bengalis are great food lovers. It is since the yester years you will be appalled to find the time and money they spent on food. Whether it is marriage ceremony or any other occasion, it’s a party time for them. Bengali food has won the hearts in all the delicacies they have presented to the world.

  • Our Mission

    Our mission is “To offer authentic Bengali food with homely feeling". Our endeavor to bring you mouth-watering traditional Bengali cuisine prepared by cooks from Bengal!

  • Our Vision

    Our mission will always be to put this "passion on to your plate" and make you a part of our family as we reach for our next milestone! The 'New age offerings,' the vibrant and warm service, the wide selection of global flavors’, makes every dining experience truly "special."
